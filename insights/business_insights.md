# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Findings

### Revenue Insights
- Net Sales increased consistently across fiscal years.
- Growth was uneven across divisions and markets.

### Profitability Insights
- Gross Margin % declined despite revenue growth.
- Indicates rising costs or pricing pressure.

### Customer Insights
- A small group of customers contributed disproportionately to revenue.
- Customer concentration risk should be monitored.

### Product Insights
- Certain products consistently outperformed others.
- Opportunity exists to optimize product portfolio.

## Recommendations

1. Focus expansion efforts on high-margin markets.
2. Improve procurement efficiency to protect margins.
3. Strengthen retention strategies for key customers.
4. Monitor profitability KPIs alongside growth KPIs.
